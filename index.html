❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Answers Before You Reach Out

The questions enterprise teams ask most often. Have another one? Just send a brief below.

💵 How do you price engagements?
I offer three models: fixed-scope projects (defined deliverable and price), monthly retainers (ongoing build + support), and hourly consulting (strategy and advisory). Every option is anchored to the ROI we define in discovery, so you always know the expected return before committing.
⏱️ How long until I see results?
Discovery wraps in days, not weeks. You'll typically see a working demo within the first 1-2 weeks, and most production systems ship in weeks rather than months - with weekly demos so there are never any surprises.
🛡️ Do you handle HIPAA, GDPR & SOC 2 compliance?
Yes. Compliance is designed into the architecture from day one, not bolted on later. I've shipped HIPAA-compliant clinical systems and GDPR/SOC 2-aligned compliance copilots for regulated enterprises across healthcare, finance, and legal.
🔒 Will my data stay secure and confidential?
Absolutely. NDAs are standard, and I offer self-hosted deployments (e.g. self-hosted n8n and private model endpoints) for full data sovereignty. Your proprietary data never leaves infrastructure you control unless you choose otherwise.
☁️ Which cloud platforms do you work with?
All three major clouds - AWS, Azure, and GCP - and I'm certified on each. I'll recommend the platform that best fits your existing stack, budget, and compliance needs rather than forcing a one-size-fits-all approach.
🔧 Do you offer support after launch?
Yes - 24/7 monitoring, maintenance, and optimisation are available via support retainers. Production AI needs drift detection, model refreshes, and ongoing tuning to keep delivering ROI, and I keep your systems performing long after go-live.
